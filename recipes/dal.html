Dal

Description

A simple Indian lentil soup

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Jeera
  • Onion
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Tomato
  • Dal
  • Green Chilly
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add jeera when oil is hot enough
  3. Chop onions and add it to the pan when jeera starts to sputter
  4. Add Chilly and saute until onions turn transclucent
  5. Add Ginger Garlic paste and let it heat till the raw smell goes away
  6. Add tomatos and cook for 2-3 mins
  7. Add all spices and cook till it becomes mushy
  8. Pressure cook dal, mash it and add to the pan
    9. \
  9. Cook on high for 2 mins, cover and simmer for 10 mins
  10. Serve hot!
